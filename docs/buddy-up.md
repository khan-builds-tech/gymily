# Gymily — Buddy Up Feature

## Idea

Find someone to train with at the gym. One tap to ask, they say yes or no.

---

## How it works

```
You ──► tap "Buddy Up" on someone ──► they get a notification
                                            │
                                  ┌─────────┴─────────┐
                                  ▼                   ▼
                               Accept              Reject
                                  │                   │
                          You're buddies!      Request closed
```

1. You see people training at your gym.
2. Tap **Buddy Up** on a person.
3. They get a notification: _"Aman wants to buddy up."_
4. They **Accept** or **Reject**.
5. If accepted, you're connected for the session — chat / find each other.

---

## Rules

- Only people **checked in at the same gym** can buddy up.
- A request can be **accepted** or **rejected** (no pressure).
- Requests expire after the session / a few hours.
- You can't spam — one pending request per person.

---

## Why people like it

- Spot partner when training alone.
- Meet regulars at your gym.
- Makes the gym feel social, not lonely.
